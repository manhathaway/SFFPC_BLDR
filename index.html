about

Hello! I’m Alex - an enthusiast that builds small-form-factor gaming PCs. I see these little machines as the future of PC gaming, as not only are they as powerful as regular-sized gaming PCs, but they’re also small enough to take them wherever you go.

Though it is a greater challenge to build these tiny beasts, I have the experience to build your next SFFPC exactly how you envision it.
